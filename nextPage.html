I would like to play Canadian Higlander but no one really plays that here. Most of this is probably just confusing for people who don't understand Magic.

In addition to those books being my favorite series, Brandon Sanderson is my favorite author. I prefer my books to have large worlds with lots of lore and detail, characters come second for me.
